About Scrum environment

  • Valuable work increments are delivered in short cycles (sprints) of one month or less.
  • Continuous feedback during the Sprint enables process inspection and adaptation.
  • The Scrum Team, composed of a Scrum Master, a Product Owner, and developers, is accountable for converting work selections into valuable increments during a Sprint.
  • The Scrum Team, alongside stakeholders, inspects Sprint results and adjusts for subsequent ones.
