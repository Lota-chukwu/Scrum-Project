Meet Our Founder

"Hello, I'm Johnbosco, a seasoned Scrum Master and Agile Consultant deeply committed to transforming workspaces into dynamic hubs of productivity. With a strong emphasis on fostering self-organized, cross-functional teams, I specialize in guiding organizations towards consistently delivering 'potentially shippable increments' with each Sprint. My approach is rooted in passion and dedication, aiming to create an environment where teams thrive and innovation flourishes.

Let's connect and embark on a journey to unleash the full potential of your team and organization. Whether it's refining Agile practices, coaching teams to reach new heights of performance, or providing strategic insights as an Agile consultant, I'm here to support your goals. Together, we can redefine what it means to be truly excellent in the realm of Agile and Scrum mastery

To get twice work done in less time, you need an excellent Scrum Master, not a good Scrum Master!

Our Mission Statement

It is such a beautiful thing reading the Agile Manifesto which states that, "We are uncovering better ways of developing software by doing it and helping others do it." One thing that makes what we do unique is that we help individuals, teams, and organizations to solve complex problems. The application of Scrum spans across many factions of life, be it in software development, politics, banking, government, even in education (no wonder we have what is called "eduScrum").
